Privacy Policy for Know Nasties Limited

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Know Nasties Limited ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the Know Nasties mobile application.

2. Data Collection & Usage

  • No Personal Data Collection: Know Nasties does not require a user account, name, or email address to function. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • On-Device Processing: All ingredient analysis is performed in real-time. Images of food labels captured by your camera are processed to identify allergens and are not uploaded to our servers or stored permanently on your device.
  • Allergen Profiles: Your chosen allergen preferences (e.g., "Peanut Allergy") are stored locally on your device only.

3. Third-Party Services

Images captured for analysis are transmitted securely to the Google Gemini API for real-time processing. These images are stored by Google temporarily (for up to 48 hours) to facilitate the analysis and for safety monitoring, after which they are automatically deleted. Google does not use these images to train its artificial intelligence models.

4. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not hold information for you to access, correct, or delete. You have full control over your data by simply deleting the app from your device.

5. Sub-processors

To provide our services, we use the following third-party sub-processors:

  • Google Cloud / Gemini API: Used for image analysis and ingredient translation. (Data processed in EU/US regions, ephemeral storage only).

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at hello@knownasties.app.
